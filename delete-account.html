MyTrade Pro — Account & Data Deletion

You can permanently delete your MyTrade Pro account and all associated data at any time, directly from within the app. No need to contact us — the deletion is instant and self-service.

How to Delete Your Account In-App

  1. Open the MyTrade Pro app on your Android device
  2. Tap the Settings tab (gear icon) at the bottom of the screen
  3. Scroll down and tap Account
  4. Tap Delete account and all data
  5. Read the confirmation message and tap Delete permanently to confirm
⚠️ This action cannot be undone. Deleting your account will permanently remove all of your data including clients, projects, quotes, bookings, and your business profile.

What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days:

  • Your name and email address (Google account information)
  • All clients and their contact details
  • All projects, notes, and job records
  • All quotes and line items
  • All bookings and calendar entries
  • Your business profile (trading name, address, VAT number, terms)
  • Your subscription status and purchase history (note: Google Play manages billing records separately)

What Is Retained

After account deletion, we do not retain any personal data. Google Play Billing may retain transaction records for legal and financial compliance purposes — this is managed by Google, not by MyTrade Pro.

Subscription Cancellation

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel your Google Play subscription. To avoid future charges, please cancel your subscription separately before deleting your account:

  1. Open the Google Play Store app
  2. Tap your profile icon → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions
  3. Find MyTrade Pro and tap Cancel subscription
