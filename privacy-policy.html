Privacy Policy

Effective date: 8 April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MyTrade Pro ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to handling your data responsibly and in compliance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Who We Are

MyTrade Pro is a mobile application designed for UK tradespeople to manage clients, jobs, quotes, bookings, and material calculations. If you have any questions about this policy or how your data is handled, please contact us at the address provided at the bottom of this page.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide the app's functionality. This includes:

  • Account information: Your name and email address, obtained when you sign in via Google OAuth. We do not store your Google password.
  • Business data you create: Client names, addresses, and contact details; project names, notes, and costs; quotes and line items; booking dates and notes; and your business profile (trading name, address, VAT number, terms).
  • Device information: Basic device identifiers used by our authentication provider to maintain your session securely.
  • Subscription status: Whether you hold an active Pro subscription, managed via Google Play Billing and RevenueCat. We do not store your payment card details.

We do not collect location data, contacts, camera images, microphone audio, or any other device permissions beyond what is listed above.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the features of the app:

  • To authenticate you securely and maintain your session across devices.
  • To store and display the clients, projects, quotes, bookings, and business profile you create.
  • To verify your subscription status and unlock Pro features accordingly.
  • To send you push notifications about bookings or reminders, if you grant notification permission.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app. We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in a secure database hosted on the Manus platform. All data is scoped to your user account — no other user can access your data. Data is transmitted over HTTPS at all times. Your session token is stored in your device's secure storage (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore) and is never stored in plain text.

5. Third-Party Services

MyTrade Pro uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Sign-In (OAuth): Used for authentication. Google Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat: Used to manage in-app subscriptions and verify purchase receipts. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing: Used to process subscription payments. Google Payments Privacy Notice

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account (via Settings → Account → Delete account and all data), all of your data — including clients, projects, quotes, bookings, and your business profile — is permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days. This action cannot be undone.

7. Your Rights Under UK GDPR

As a UK resident, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access: You can request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: You can correct inaccurate data directly within the app.
  • Right to erasure: You can delete your account and all associated data at any time via Settings → Account.
  • Right to data portability: You can request an export of your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to object: You can object to any processing of your data that you believe is not justified.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

MyTrade Pro is intended for use by adults operating trade businesses. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under the age of 18. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. For significant changes, we will notify you via the app.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us at:

  • App: MyTrade Pro
  • Email: privacy@mytradepro.app (update with your actual email)

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk if you believe your data has been handled unlawfully.
